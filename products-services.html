Product Overview

The trains are newer, so shouldn't the service be better? Upon researching, we found out that the real problem with the inadequate train service is the decades-old train track system. Thus, MetroLive was born. Our software engineers write software that tackles issues with track signaling. But, these changes cannot be made with the current signaling system, so our hardware engineers are constantly working to upgrade the outdated system, allowing our new software to run smoothly and effectively.

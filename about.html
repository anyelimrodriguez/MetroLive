Who We Are

As New York City residents, the MetroLive team has experienced firsthand the frustrating difficulties with the MTA during our daily subway commutes. From delays to safety concerns, the current MTA system does not address the problems voiced by many travelers. As a result, MetroLive was created. With our software, we provide a solution to a problem that has persisted for decades.

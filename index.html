We're Here to FIX the MTA.

The Problem

The current MTA system is unreliable in terms of estimated time arrival and punctuality. The results of a survey conducted in 2017 show that “among respondents who were employed, subway delays caused 74 percent to be late for a work meeting, 18 percent to be reprimanded, and 13 percent to lose wages". Subway riders also found on-train announcements ineffective in announcing delays and other important information, grading their announcement system a “C” or lower!

Our Solution

MetroLive will develop state of the art software that will enhance busy New Yorker's experience with the MTA. We will upgrade obsolete mechanical equipment installed as long as 80 years ago to the latest version of hardware. Our technology will make trains faster AND punctual.
